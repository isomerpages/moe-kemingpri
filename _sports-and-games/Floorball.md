<p>The Floorball CCA aims to cultivate teamwork and resilience for both boys and girls through the rigours of tough yet fun training. The CCA also strives to develop leadership and passion for the sport, with many members pursuing their floorball aspirations even after they have graduated from Keming, and achieving stellar results.</p>
