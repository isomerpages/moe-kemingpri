<h4><strong>Curricular Goal</strong></h4>
<p>To equip our students with the physical skills, attitudes and values to enjoy a physically active and healthy life.</p>
<p><strong><u>Our Programmes &amp; Activities</u></strong></p>
<p>Through our programmes and activities, we want our students to:</p>
<p>1) Develop and maintain physical health and fitness through regular participation in physical activities</p>
<p>2) Understand and apply movement concepts within physical activities</p>
<p>3) Demonstrate awareness of safe practices during physical and daily activities</p>
<p>4) Espoused the spirit of sportsmanship, friendship, respect, resilience and fair play.</p>
<p><strong><u>PE Department Programmes &amp; Activities</u></strong></p>
<p><strong>Term 1:<br /></strong>Height &amp; Weight Measurement (all levels)<br />P3 Swimsafer Programme<br />Level Sports Day for Primary 4</p>
<p><strong>Term 2:<br /></strong>Primary 4 Outdoor Adventure Camp (3 days, 2 nights)<br />Primary 6 NAPFA Test<br />Sports Enrichment Programme for P4</p>
<p><strong>Term 3:<br /></strong>Height &amp; Weight Measurement (all levels)<br />Primary 4 NAPFA Test<br />Lower Primary Games Day (Primary 1 &amp; 2)<br />Level Sports Day for Primary 3<br />Level Sports Day for Primary 5<br />Sports Enrichment Programme for P3</p>
<p><strong>Term 4:<br /></strong>Level Sports Day for Primary 6<br />Sports Enrichment Programme for P5</p>
