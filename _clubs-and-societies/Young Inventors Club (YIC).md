<p>The main aim of Young Inventors Club is to develop students' interest
in creativity and to prepare them to be future-ready innovators. The Club
strongly supports the development of 21st Century Competencies and trains
each individual student to be a:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Y</strong>oung empathetic innovator</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>I</strong>nquisitive thinker</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>C</strong>ommitted contributor</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The Club introduces students to Design Thinking strategies, develops their
skills in problem-solving, critical thinking and project presentations
and challenges them to create solutions that are beneficial and related
to the users who will be using their inventions. Learning journeys are
also organized to expand studentsâ€™ horizon in the field of designing</p>
